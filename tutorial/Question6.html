Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 80g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 2 stalks curry leaves

Cooking Steps:

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant.
  2. Add in chicken and continue saute until chicken realesed oil.
  3. Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
  4. Chicken simmer in low fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  5. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  6. Remove and serve
