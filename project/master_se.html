Bachelor in Computer Science (Hons.)

Programme Overview

The Master in Software Engineering programme enhances students’ understanding of advanced software systems, design patterns, and emerging technologies. It combines research, management, and technical knowledge. This programme takes 2 years, including research work and a professional project or internship to build real-world expertise.

Programme Objectives

  • To build expertise in advanced software design and development.
  • To prepare students for leadership roles in IT projects.
  • To encourage innovation through research in software technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Senior Software Engineer
  • Technical Consultant
  • Software Architect
  • Project Leader
