Admission& Application

We welcome students who are passionate about technology and innovation. Follow the steps below to apply for our programmes.

Admission Requirements

  • SPM / O-Level with minimum 3 credits.
  • Credit in English and Mathematics.
  • English proficiency recommended.
  • Interest in computing, technology, and problem-solving.

Application Steps

  1. Check entry requirements.
  2. Prepare all the required documents (Academic Certificate, IC/Passport, Picture).
  3. Complete the online application form below.
  4. Submit your documents to our email admission@techbridge.edu.my
  5. Wait for the confirmation email from our admission team.
