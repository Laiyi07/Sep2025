Bachelor in Computer Science (Hons.)

Programme Overview

The Bachelor in Computer Science programme offers deep knowledge in software design, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and computer systems. Students gain hands-on experience through projects and industrial training. It is a 3-year programme including 1 semester of industrial training to enhance students’ professional development.

Programme Objectives

  • To equip students with strong programming and analytical skills.
  • To encourage innovation in technology-based problem solving.
  • To prepare graduates for careers or postgraduate studies in computing.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • AI Developer
  • System Analyst
