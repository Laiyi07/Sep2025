Bachelor in Computer Science (Hons.)

Programme Overview

This programme focuses on software development life cycle, including design, testing, and maintenance. Students learn modern frameworks, project management, and quality assurance techniques. The duration is 3 years with the final semester dedicated to industrial training where students gain real work experience.

Programme Objectives

  • To develop strong problem-solving and project management skills.
  • To train students in teamwork and real-world software projects.
  • To prepare graduates for roles in software development companies.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Engineer
  • System Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Quality Assurance Engineer
