Diploma in Computer Science

Programme Overview

The Diploma in Computer Science provides students with a solid foundation in programming, problem-solving, and software development. Students will learn popular languages such as Python, Java, and C++, and gain practical skills in databases and web technologies. This programme takes 2 years of study and 5 months of industrial training to prepare students for real-world experience.

Programme Objectives

  • To provide fundamental knowledge of programming and computing concepts.
  • To train students to design and develop computer software efficiently.
  • To prepare students for further studies in IT-related degrees.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Web Programmer
  • IT Support Technician
  • Junior Database Administrator
