Diploma in Computer Science

Programme Overview

This programme focuses on computer systems, networking, databases, and cybersecurity. Students will learn how to maintain IT infrastructure, manage information systems, and apply technology to real-world problems. The programme includes 2 years of classroom study and 5 months of industrial training to strengthen practical knowledge.

Programme Objectives

  • To develop students’ understanding of IT systems and operations.
  • To train students to manage networks and databases efficiently.
  • To prepare students for technical roles in the IT industry.

Career Opportunities

  • Network Technician
  • IT Support Executive
  • Database Assistant
  • System Administrator
