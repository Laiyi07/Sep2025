Bachelor in Computer Science (Hons.)

Programme Overview

This programme focuses on data analytics, machine learning, and data visualization. Students learn to manage and analyze large datasets to support business and scientific decision-making. It is a 2-year programme with a research project or internship in the final semester for practical experience.

Programme Objectives

  • To provide advanced knowledge of data science and analytics tools.
  • To train students to apply machine learning and AI techniques.
  • To prepare graduates for research or data-driven careers.

Career Opportunities

  • Data Scientist
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • AI Specialist
