About Us

TechBridge College focuses on computing and information technology education. We provided programs such as computer science, software engineering, and information technology. Our programs are designed to help students gain knowledge and practical skills in computing.

We aim to prepare students for future careers in technology by providing hands-on learning and guidance from experienced lecturers. The School of Computing encourages innovation, problem-solving, and lifelong learning.
